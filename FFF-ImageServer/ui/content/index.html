Grafficities

Introduction

Due to the popularity of covid-19, people have to work remotely, keep social distancing and have difficulty traveling to a location to view it in person. The team's founders took note of this and aimed to create an online graffiti art-themed website that would allow users to remotely visit colorful graffiti around the world and create their own canvases that could be posted on a live map for more people to see.

Don't forget to save your work!

About Grafficities


Grafficities, named after the concepts of graffiti and city, is dedicated to creating a graffiti city map where more people can create, explore, and communicate without regard to distance.
